# Huong Dan Su Dung - UME Pet Salon

## 1. Dang ky / Dang nhap
- Truy cap trang web -> Nhan Dang ky
- Dien email, mat khau, ho ten -> Dang ky
- Hoac dang nhap bang Google/Facebook

## 2. Mua san pham
- Vao trang San pham -> Chon san pham -> Them vao gio
- Vao Gio hang -> Kiem tra -> Nhan Thanh toan
- Dien dia chi giao hang -> Xac nhan don hang

## 3. Dat lich dich vu
- Vao trang Dich vu -> Chon dich vu
- Nhan Dat lich -> Chon ngay/gio/nhan vien
- Xac nhan dat lich

## 4. Quan ly thu cung
- Vao Thu cung cua toi -> Them thu cung moi
- Dien thong tin: ten, loai, giong, tuoi...
- Co the dang ban/nhan nuoi tren Marketplace

## 5. AI nhan dien thu cung
- Vao trang Nhan dien thu cung
- Upload anh thu cung -> He thong tu nhan dien
- Hien thi: loai thu cung, giong, do chinh xac

## 6. AI Chatbot
- Nhan icon chat o goc duoi phai
- Hoi bat ky cau hoi ve cham soc thu cung
- Chatbot se tu van va goi y dich vu/san pham
